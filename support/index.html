MedLedger Support

Help, contact, and basic troubleshooting for MedLedger.

Contact

For app support, bug reports, general feedback, or feature requests, email the developer at petermzc@icloud.com.

Please include your device model, iOS version, MedLedger app version, and a short description of the issue.

Common Help Topics

  • If reminders are not appearing, confirm that notifications are enabled for MedLedger in the iOS Settings app.
  • If Shortcuts or NFC logging is not working, open the Shortcuts app and confirm that your automation is enabled and points to the correct MedLedger medication.
  • If dose history looks different than expected, check whether the medication schedule, weekdays, or dose times were recently edited.
  • If inventory or refill timing looks wrong, review the medication's current quantity and recurring schedule in MedLedger.

Privacy

MedLedger stores medication and log information locally on your device and does not collect user data. Read the full Privacy Policy.
