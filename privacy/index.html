MedLedger Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 23, 2026

Summary

MedLedger does not collect, transmit, sell, rent, or share personal information. The app is designed to store your medication tracking information locally on your device.

Information Stored On Your Device

MedLedger lets you enter and manage medication-related information, including medication names, strengths, schedules, dose logs, inventory and refill settings, reminder preferences, and notes.

This information is stored locally on your device using Apple's on-device app storage technologies. The developer does not operate a server for MedLedger and does not have access to the information you enter in the app.

No Data Collection

MedLedger does not collect data from the app. In particular, MedLedger does not use:

  • User accounts or sign-in.
  • Analytics services.
  • Advertising networks.
  • Tracking technologies.
  • Third-party data brokers.
  • CloudKit sync or a developer-operated cloud database.

Notifications And Shortcuts

If you enable reminders, MedLedger schedules local notifications on your device. Notification scheduling is handled by iOS and is not sent to the developer.

If you use MedLedger with Shortcuts or NFC automations, the selected medication action runs on your device. The developer does not receive the details of those automations or the medication information used by them.

Device Backups

Depending on your Apple device settings, app data stored on your device may be included in Apple-managed device backups, such as encrypted computer backups or iCloud backups. These backups are controlled by Apple and your device settings. The developer does not receive or control those backups.

Children's Privacy

Because MedLedger does not collect personal information, it does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes To This Policy

If MedLedger's privacy practices change, this policy will be updated before those changes are reflected in the app.

Contact
